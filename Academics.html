Course We Offer

We are offer to three best computer science courses for student by VIT University

BTech

The BTech course is a full time four years (8 semesters) Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science.The degree helps interested students in setting up a sound academic base for an advanced career in Computer Science.

MTech

Mtech in CSE is a full time two years (4 semesters) Master’s Degree in Computer Science. basically, it is about software Engineering and Development. This Post-Graduate Degree is focused on subjects such as software, databases, and networking.

Mtech - Integrated

Mtech-Integrated in CSE is a full time five years (10 semesters) Master’s Degree in Computer Science. basically, it is a Integrated course giving a direct PG degree. This Post-Graduate Degree is focused on subjects such as software Development, databases, and networking.

MSc - Integrated

An MSc Integrated program is a comprehensive academic program designed to provide students with an advanced and integrated education in a specific field, typically in science, technology, or other specialized areas. Unlike traditional Master's programs, MSc Integrated programs are usually longer in duration and offer a more in-depth curriculum.

BArch

Bachelor of Architecture, is a full-time undergraduate degree program typically spanning five years (10 semesters) that focuses on the study and practice of architecture. This degree is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in architectural theory, design principles, construction technology, and practical skills necessary for a career in architecture.
